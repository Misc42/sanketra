Privacy Policy

Sanketra — Smart Remote  |  Last updated: February 15, 2026

Sanketra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All communication stays within your local network between your phone and your own computer.

What Sanketra Does

Sanketra turns your Android phone into a smart remote for your computer. It provides:

  • Voice-to-text input (speech recognition runs on your own computer)
  • Trackpad and gyroscope-based cursor control
  • Screen mirroring from your computer to your phone
  • Remote server setup via SSH

Permissions & Why We Need Them

Microphone — Captures your voice for speech-to-text. Audio is streamed directly to your computer over your local Wi-Fi network. Audio is never sent to any cloud service, third-party server, or the internet.

Internet / Wi-Fi — Used solely for local network (LAN) communication between your phone and your computer. Sanketra connects to a server running on your own machine within your home or office network.

Foreground Service — Keeps audio recording active when the app is in the background or the screen is off, so voice input continues working uninterrupted.

Wake Lock & Wi-Fi Lock — Prevents the phone from dropping the Wi-Fi connection or sleeping during an active session, ensuring reliable real-time communication.

Motion Sensors (Gyroscope) — Used for gyroscope pointer mode, where tilting your phone controls the cursor on your computer screen. Sensor data is sent only to your computer over your local network.

Data Handling

  • Audio data is streamed in real-time to your own computer for speech recognition. It is not recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere else.
  • Touch and motion data (trackpad gestures, gyroscope orientation) is sent to your computer for cursor control. It is not logged or stored.
  • SSH credentials (if used for remote setup) are stored locally on your device in Android's encrypted storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by Android Keystore). They are never transmitted to any third party.
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device in encrypted storage and are used only to communicate with your own computer.

No Data Collection

Sanketra does not:

  • Collect or store personal information
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Transmit any data to cloud servers or third parties
  • Require account creation or sign-in
  • Access contacts, photos, location, or any data unrelated to its function

Network Security

All communication between your phone and computer uses TLS encryption (HTTPS/WSS). The app uses a trust-on-first-use (TOFU) certificate pinning model to verify your computer's identity and prevent man-in-the-middle attacks on your local network.

Children's Privacy

Sanketra is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
